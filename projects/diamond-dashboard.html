Diamond Dashboard Analysis

Interactive Power BI dashboards comparing lab-grown and natural diamonds for industry professionals.

Tech Stack
Power BI Python Excel R Power Automate
Category
Data Analytics Business Intelligence Dashboard

Overview

This project developed comprehensive interactive dashboards to analyze and compare lab-grown versus natural diamonds across multiple dimensions. Using Power BI and advanced data visualization techniques, the dashboards provide diamond industry professionals with insights into pricing trends, quality distributions, and market dynamics between these two categories of diamonds.

The diamond industry has undergone significant transformation with the introduction of lab-grown diamonds. This dashboard solution bridges the analytical gap by providing a data-driven platform for comparing these two product categories across various metrics, enabling better decision-making for retailers, wholesalers, and investors.

Problem

The diamond industry has been disrupted by the growing market for lab-created diamonds, but there was a lack of analytical tools to properly compare these products with natural diamonds. Industry stakeholders needed a data-driven approach to understand price differentials, quality variations, consumer preferences, and market trends between lab and natural diamonds to make informed business decisions.

  • Lack of standardized comparison tools between lab-grown and natural diamonds
  • Difficulty tracking rapidly changing price differentials across diamond categories
  • Limited visibility into market share trends and consumer preference shifts
  • Challenges in forecasting future price movements for inventory planning
  • Need for accessible insights for both technical and non-technical stakeholders

Solution

The solution was a comprehensive Power BI dashboard system that integrates data from multiple industry sources to provide real-time comparisons and insights. The system follows a data pipeline architecture starting with automated data collection from various sources, followed by preprocessing in Python and Excel. The cleaned data is then loaded into Power BI for visualization and analysis.

Interactive dashboard showing price comparison between lab-grown and natural diamonds

Power Automate handles the scheduled refreshes and report distribution. The dashboards are designed with a modular approach, allowing users to drill down from high-level overviews to detailed comparisons based on specific diamond attributes such as carat, cut, clarity, and color.

Process

Data Collection

Gathered diamond pricing and attribute data from multiple industry sources, including wholesale platforms, retail channels, and market reports.

Data Processing

Used Python and Excel for data cleaning, transformation, and standardization to ensure consistent comparison metrics across different data sources.

Statistical Analysis

Applied R for advanced statistical comparisons and predictive modeling to identify trends and forecast future price movements.

Dashboard Development

Created interactive Power BI dashboards with drill-down capabilities, custom visualizations, and user-friendly interfaces.

Automation Setup

Implemented Power Automate workflows for scheduled data refreshes, report distribution, and alert notifications for significant market changes.

Key Features

Price Comparison

Interactive charts comparing lab-grown and natural diamond prices by carat, cut, clarity, and color.

Market Share Analysis

Time-series visualizations showing market share trends between lab-grown and natural diamonds.

Quality Distribution

Comparative analysis of quality distributions across different diamond categories.

Geographic Insights

Regional market analysis showing consumer preferences and pricing variations by location.

Predictive Analytics

Price trend forecasting using statistical models to predict future market movements.

Automated Reporting

Scheduled report generation and distribution with Excel integration for further analysis.

Challenges Faced

  • Harmonizing data from disparate sources with different grading standards
  • Creating meaningful comparisons between products with fundamentally different origins
  • Designing intuitive visualizations for complex multi-dimensional data
  • Implementing real-time data updates from rapidly changing market conditions
  • Balancing technical depth with accessibility for non-technical users
